Editing







Kate's ten years of experience in editing include positions at W.W. Norton, Cambridge University Press, Island Press, the National Science Teaching Association, and the literary magazine The G.W. Review. In these capacities, she gained extensive experience in editing scholarly, trade (fiction and nonfiction), and professional publications. She is also skilled at reviewing resumes, cover letters, career and academic applications, and academic papers. She currently works as a freelance copyeditor and proofreader and is accepting new clients.

Kate has also written grants for nonprofit organizations such as 826DC and NSTA. She is currently accepting new grantwriting clients, as well.
