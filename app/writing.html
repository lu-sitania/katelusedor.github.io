Writing







Kate writes fiction. She is currently working on a collection of short stories and a novel set in Gilded Age New York.

She is especially interested in using genre tropes as metaphors for talking about racial, ethnic, and socioeconomic difference; cross-media storytelling; and interactive narratives, including Twine games and video games.
